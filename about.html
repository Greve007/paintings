I practise several types of paintings, such as charcoal, aquarel and ink pen. The paintings and drawings are made from home, where I work on the house and garden. Apart from portrets, which take up most of my work, I also paint marine and coastal scapes and other works from time to time.

Currently, I am using charcoal to draw portrets of intriguing faces. It would be a dream of mine to draw more and sell them to people that appreciate the art. If you are interested, please contact me through my email.
